Emmys: Allison Janney Wins Supporting Actress in a Comedy

Allison Janney was victorious on Emmys Monday. 

"This is an amazing honor, thank you. Number six, let's go," she said during her acceptance speech for the supporting actress in a comedy category.

The actress previously claimed a fifth Emmy win at the Creative Arts Emmys on Aug. 16 for her role as a guest actress in the Showtime series Masters of Sex. 

The actress beat out Mayim Bialik (Orange Is the New Black), Julie Bowen (Modern Family), Anna Chlumsky (Veep), Kate McKinnon (Saturday Night Live) and Kate Mulgrew (Orange Is the New Black) in the category.
