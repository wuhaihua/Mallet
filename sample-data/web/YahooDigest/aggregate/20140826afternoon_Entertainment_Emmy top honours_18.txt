Emmys 2014: Complete Winners List from the 66th Primetime Emmy Awards

Going for the gold! Television's biggest stars hit the Nokia Theatre in Los Angeles on Monday, Aug. 25 for the 2014 Emmy Awards, competing for the major TV honor.

The nominations for the 66th Primetime Emmy Awards were led by HBO's Game of Thrones, which received 19 nominations, including nods for Outstanding Drama Series, Outstanding Writing for a Drama Series, and Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for Peter Dinklage. Other series at the top of the pile at the beginning of the night included FX's Fargo and AMC's Breaking Bad. 

PHOTOS: Emmy Awards 2014 Red Carpet: What the Stars Wore

Hosted by Seth Meyers, the 2014 Emmys boasted famous presenters, such as Halle Berry, Sofia Vergara, Matthew McConaughey, and Jimmy Fallon. Top nominees for the night included Scandal's Kerry Washington, House of Cards' Kevin Spacey, Parks and Recreation's Amy Poehler, and The Big Bang Theory's Jim Parsons.
