Wizards Of The Coast Reveals New D&D Video Games Wizards Of The Coast Reveals New D&D Video Games The publisher of the fantasy role-playing game Dungeons &amp; Dragons revealed new plans for tie-in video games at the Penny Arcade Expo in Seattle, Washington this weekend, including a new “castle defense” mobile game and “enhanced re-releases” of several classic titles. 
Renton, Washington-based Wizards of the Coast (a subsidiary of Hasbro) made three major announcements at a PAX Prime panel called 2015 Videogames in the Forgotten Realms:
	
								 
Neverwinter MMORPG on Xbox One The free-to-play MMORPG Neverwinter will be coming to Microsoft’s Xbox One console in the first half of 2015. At launch, console gamers will be able to experience the fast-paced combat and epic dungeons all set in the Dungeons &amp; Dragons Forgotten Realms universe. 
New D&amp;D Game From Backflip Studios  Colorado-based Backflip Studios is designing a “castle defense” style D&amp;D game for mobile. It is set to release in 2015 and additional information will be announced in the coming months.
								
										 
Beamdog Releasing More “Enhanced” Games Digital distribution system Beamdog will release an enhanced edition of Icewind Dale for iOS, Android and PC later this year, and there are two updates coming for the Baldur’s Gate: Enhanced Edition, including availability on iPhone and cross-platform multiplayer. 
Earlier this month, Wizards of the Coast released long-anticipated new rules for the Dungeons &amp; Dragons tabletop role-playing game, and early reports indicate they’ve been big sellers.
