GMR Infra signs MoU with JBIC GMR Infra signs MoU with JBIC 
Bangalore-based GMR Infrastructure Limited has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) for providing financial assistance to Japanese companies investing in Infrastructure projects of GMR Group.


MoU was signed during the course of the state visit of the Prime Minister Narendra Modi's to Japan.


The objective of this MoU is to provide low-cost long-duration financing for infrastructure development projects that involve Japanese companies in development of industrial parks, power, energy, ports, airports, highways and railway projects.
