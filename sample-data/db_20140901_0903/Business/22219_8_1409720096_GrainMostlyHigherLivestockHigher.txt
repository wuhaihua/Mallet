Grain Mostly Higher, Livestock Higher Grain Mostly Higher, Livestock Higher Grain futures were mostly higher Monday in early trading on the Chicago Board of Trade.Wheat for Dec delivery was unchanged at $5.6350 a bushel; Dec corn was 1.25 cents lower at $3.6350 a bushel; Dec oats were unchanged at $3.4225 a bushel; while Nov soybeans gained 9.75 cents to $10.34 a bushel.Beef and pork higher on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange.Oct live cattle was 1.58 cents higher at $1.5300 pound; Oct feeder cattle was 2.53 cents higher at 2.1925 a pound; Oct lean hogs rose 1.98 cents to $1.0010 a pound.
