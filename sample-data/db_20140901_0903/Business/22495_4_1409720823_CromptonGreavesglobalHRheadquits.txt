Crompton Greaves' global HR head quits Crompton Greaves' global HR head quits 
Crompton Greaves said Sanjay Jorapur, Executive Vice-President and Global Head-Human Resources, has decided to pursue his career outside the group.


In a filing to the stock exchange,the company said a new HR group head has been recruited and till he takes charge, Sanjay Sen, HR Head, Avantha Group, will oversee the function.
