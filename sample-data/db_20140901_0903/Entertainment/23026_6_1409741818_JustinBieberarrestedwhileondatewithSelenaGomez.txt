Justin Bieber arrested while on date with Selena Gomez Justin Bieber arrested while on date with Selena Gomez 
					Toronto: Singer Justin Bieber was recently taken into police custody during an outing with his on and off girlfriend and pop star Selena Gomez in Canada.Bieber got into a physical altercation with the driver of a mini van after the singer's all-terrain vehicle collided with the mini van in Stratford, Ontario 29 Aug. The star was arrested and then released on a promise to appear in court 29 Sep, reports aceshowbiz.com.From the pictures that surfaced on the internet, Bieber was seen riding his all-terrain vehicle with Gomez before the accident which was described as non-major and no one suffered injuries. No charges were made against the other driver.IANS
