Rahul Gandhi to visit Amethi on Thursday Rahul Gandhi to visit Amethi on Thursday 
					Lucknow: Congress vice president Rahul Gandhi will visit his parliamentary constituency Amethi for two days beginning Thursday.Gandhi will meet villagers in the Uttar Pradesh constituency and interact with Congress workers at the Munshiganj guest house where he will stay, party sources said on Tuesday.Congress president Sonia Gandhi also visited her parliamentary constituency Raebareli and met party workers and villagers Monday. She raised questions about the promises being made by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.This is Sonia's maiden visit to Raebareli after the polls debacle in May.&nbsp;IANS
