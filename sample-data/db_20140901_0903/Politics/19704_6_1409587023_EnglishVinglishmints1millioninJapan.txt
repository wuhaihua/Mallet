'English Vinglish' mints $1 million in Japan 'English Vinglish' mints $1 million in Japan 
					New Delhi: Sridevi's Bollywood comeback vehicle "English Vinglish" has registered a box office collection of $1 million in Japan. The veteran actress is elated.The movie, backed by Eros International, continues to perform well at the box office after hitting theatres in Japan 28 June.After releasing in 33 screens initially, the film opened to audiences in another 17 odd screens additionally, taking the total screen count to 50 in across 20 cities in Japan, said a statement.Sridevi is happy with the dream run of the movie, which tells a moving tale of a home-bound woman who doesn't know English and how an overseas trip helps her to bring out the best in her.&ldquo;I am flattered with all the love and affection the film continues to receive and once again thank the director Gauri Shinde for making such a wonderful film," she said.The grand premiere of the film was held in Tokyo 27 May.IANS
