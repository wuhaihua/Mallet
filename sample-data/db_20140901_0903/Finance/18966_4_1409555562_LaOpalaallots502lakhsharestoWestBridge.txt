La Opala allots 5.02 lakh shares to WestBridge La Opala allots 5.02 lakh shares to WestBridge 
The allotment committee constituted by the board of directors of La Opala RG Ltd has allotted 5.02 lakh shares of Rs 10 each to WestBridge Crossover Fund, LLC, at Rs 1,100 a share. 

Shareholders of the company had approved the proposal on August 27. The stock is currently ruling at Rs 1.343 on the NSE.
