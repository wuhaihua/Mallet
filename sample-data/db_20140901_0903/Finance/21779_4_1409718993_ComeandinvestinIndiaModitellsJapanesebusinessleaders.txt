Come and invest in India, Modi tells Japanese business leaders Come and invest in India, Modi tells Japanese business leaders 
       
       
    
   

Prime Minister Narendra Modi today told the Japanese business leaders that he does not think that there is any place better than India for them. 

"There is no Red Tape but Red Carpet in India. We have eased off lot of regulations,'' he said. 

"Come and invest in India: only place you can find democracy, demography and demand,'' he added.
