Gammon Infra QIP issue Gammon Infra QIP issue 
Gammon Infrastructure Projects has informed the stock exchange that its QIP (qualified institutional placement) committee of directors had approved the opening of QIP on September 1.


Further, the committee also resolved that the floor price of the issue is Rs 13.3375 per equity share and that the members of the committee were authorised to offer a discount of no more than five per cent on the floor price.
