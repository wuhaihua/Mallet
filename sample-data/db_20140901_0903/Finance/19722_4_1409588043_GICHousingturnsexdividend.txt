GIC Housing turns ex-dividend GIC Housing turns ex-dividend 
       
       
    
   

Shares of GIC Housing Finance will turn ex-dividend from Tuesday. As a result, those buying the shares from Tuesday will not be eligible to receive the payout. It announced a one-time special dividend of &#8377;1 towards its Silver Jubilee, along with a regular dividend of &#8377;5. The shares have a face value of &#8377;10. The company&#8217;s books will remain closed between September 4 and 11 for dividend finalisation. This is the highest payout made by GIC Housing in a year. Last year, it had paid &#8377;5.
