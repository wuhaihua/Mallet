Harvick nearly hit squirrel in Oral-B 500 Harvick nearly hit squirrel in Oral-B 500 Kevin Harvick was leading the pack at the Oral-B USA 500 in Atlanta when he was momentarily distracted by a furry four-legged creature scurrying across the track.
The creature escaped unscathed, but no one was really sure what it was.
 
Was it a cat? a squirrel? A RACCOON?
No, sources confirm that it was, in fact, a squirrel.
