								Blind completes United move
							
						
					
				
			
			Ajax have confirmed that Daley Blind has completed his move to Manchester United.The 24-year-old Netherlands international, who can play as a defensive midfielder or on the left side of a back three, has put pen to paper on a deal at Old Trafford after agreeing personal terms and passing a medical over the weekend.The two clubs agreed a £13.8million fee for Blind on Friday. Blind will link up once again with former Netherlands boss Louis van Gaal at Old Trafford.
