Mandatory Credit: Kevin Liles-USA TODAY SportsNASCAR    Tony Stewart is returning to NASCAR for the first time since a fatal accident involving his car earlier this month.  Stewart&rsquo;s return comes at Atlanta Motor Speedway for the Oral-B USA 500. While it was going to be interesting to see how Stewart was received for his return to the track after his car struck and killed 20-year-old sprint car driver Kevin Ward Jr., it appeared that the NASCAR fans were happy to have Stewart back and he was welcomed with open arms. As Stewart made his way to his race car for the start of the race, he was signing some autographs for fans before turning the last couple away.  Stewart will be starting the race in the No. 12 spot.
