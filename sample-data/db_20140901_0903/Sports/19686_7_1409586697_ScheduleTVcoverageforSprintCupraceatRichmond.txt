Schedule, TV coverage for Sprint Cup race at Richmond Schedule, TV coverage for Sprint Cup race at Richmond 
                
                
           
                    The NASCAR Chase for the Sprint Cup field will be set Saturday night at Richmond International Raceway.

And teams will have a lot of practice for it.

NASCAR has more than four hours of practice scheduled for Sprint Cup teams at Richmond to allow teams to prepare for the final race to make the 16-driver Chase for the Sprint Cup field.



The Nationwide Series also is in action this weekend at Richmond while the trucks have the weekend off.

Here is the Federated Auto Parts 400 schedule:

Thursday, September 4

Nationwide practice, 1-2 p.m. (no TV)

Nationwide practice, 2:30-4 p.m. (no TV)

Friday, September 5

Sprint Cup practice, 9 a.m.-Noon (no TV)

Sprint Cup practice, 1-2:20 p.m. (ESPN2)

Nationwide qualifying, 3:40 p.m. (ESPN2)

Sprint Cup qualifying, 5:40 p.m. (ESPN2)

Nationwide race (250 laps, 187.5 miles), 7:30 p.m. (ESPN2)

Saturday, September 6

Sprint Cup race (400 laps, 300 miles), 7:30 p.m. (ABC)
