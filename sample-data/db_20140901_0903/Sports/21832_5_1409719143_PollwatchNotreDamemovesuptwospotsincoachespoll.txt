Poll watch: Notre Dame moves up two spots in coaches poll Poll watch: Notre Dame moves up two spots in coaches poll  Despite the uncertain status of five players implicated in the university's academic investigation, Notre Dame moved up two spots to No. 15 in the USA Today coaches poll released Tuesday.
Notre Dame's comprehensive 48-17 win over Rice clearly impressed the coaches, with Everett Golson dazzling in his return to the Irish on Saturday.

Five Irish opponents rank in the top 25: No. 1 Florida State, No. 10 Stanford, No. 14 USC, No. 16 Arizona State and No. 23 North Carolina. Louisville received the 28th-most votes and Michigan received the 30th-most votes.
