Skipper Chigumbura leads Zimbabwe to a historic win over Australia Skipper Chigumbura leads Zimbabwe to a historic win over Australia 
										
		Captain Elton Chigumbura hit a fluent half-century as Zimbabwe secured a shock three-wicket win over Australia, only their second ever victory over the four-times world champions, in the triangular series on Sunday.

After electing to bat, Australia were restricted to 209 for nine in their 50 overs, a target Zimbabwe reached with two overs to spare as Chigumbura finished unbeaten on 52.
