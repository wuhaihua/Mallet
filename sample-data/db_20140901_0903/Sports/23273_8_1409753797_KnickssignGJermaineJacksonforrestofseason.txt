Knicks sign G Jermaine Jackson for rest of season Knicks sign G Jermaine Jackson for rest of season 
NEW YORK - The     New York Knicks signed reserve guard Jermaine Jackson for the remainder of the season on Sunday.  Jackson was originally signed to the first of two 10-day contracts on February 28.  The Knicks had to either sign Jackson for the rest of the year or release him by Sunday, when his second 10-day contract expired.  In just four games with the Knicks, Jackson averaged 1.8 points, 1.3 rebounds and 7.0 minutes.
