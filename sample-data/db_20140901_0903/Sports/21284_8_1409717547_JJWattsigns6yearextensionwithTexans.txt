J.J. Watt signs 6-year extension with Texans J.J. Watt signs 6-year extension with Texans ESPN's Chris Mortensen is reporting Houston Texans defensive end J.J. Watt has agreed to a 6-year, $100 million extension with the team.The deal makes Watt the highest paid defensive player in the NFL, earning him $16.6 million a season through 2021.Mortensen reports the contract contains $51.8 million guaranteed, the most for a defensive player in the history of the NFL.Watt was the 11th overall pick in the 2011 NFL entry draft. He was the NFL's defensive player of the year in 2012.
