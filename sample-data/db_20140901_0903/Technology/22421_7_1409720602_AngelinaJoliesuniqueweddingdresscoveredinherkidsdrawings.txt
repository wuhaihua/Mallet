Angelina Jolie's unique wedding dress, covered in her kids drawings Angelina Jolie's unique wedding dress, covered in her kids drawings Wedding pictures of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt grace this week's edition of People magazine, including a stunning full-length image of the 39-yr-old actress' wedding dress adorned with dozens of her children's designs and drawings sewn into the back of the gown and veil. 

The sleeveless, floor-length custom Atelier Versace gown was crafted by Luigi Massi, and apparently Donatella Versace had a hand in it as well,  according to People and the UK's Hello magazine.

Brad Pitt and Jolie married at their estate in France on August 23.
