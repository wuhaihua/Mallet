First photo from Dwyane Wade and Gabrielle Union's wedding First photo from Dwyane Wade and Gabrielle Union's wedding People magazine got an exclusive photo from the wedding ceremony of Miami Heat star Dwyane Wade and actress Gabrielle Union on Saturday. 

The couple kept the wedding under tight wraps, reportedly held at a castle in South Florida, asking guests not to bring phones or cameras.

A few photos were posted on Instagram from guests, Miami teammate Chris Bosh and Miami heat owner, Micky Arison.

Union posted a photo of the couple on Instagram a few days before the ceremony with the quote, "Let's gooooooo! #thewadeunion."
