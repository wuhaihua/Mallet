					                                                                          The man who inspired the viral ice bucket challenge, which raises money to fight Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS or Lou Gehrig's Disease), is a new dad. Pete Frates announced Sunday he and his wife, Julie, have a baby girl. &quot;Little Lucy Frates has arrived and Mom, Dad and Lucy are perfect,&quot; Frates wrote on his Facebook page. Frates is a former Boston College baseball player who was diagnosed with the degenerative disease ALS in 2012, prompting his family and friends to come up with a creative way to get people excited about raising money for ALS research.The ice bucket challenge gained incredible momentum through social media and contributed to the ALS Association raising more than $100 million during the month of August.
