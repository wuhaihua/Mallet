LaGuardia Airport Reports 3 Bird Strikes in 1 Hour LaGuardia Airport Reports 3 Bird Strikes in 1 Hour 
 

Three planes have reported bird strikes within a one-hour period at New York's LaGuardia Airport.

WABC (         http://7ny.tv/1lzcQDo ) says all three planes were arriving and landed safely without any damage. No injuries are reported.

The incidents occurred from around 7:45 a.m. to 8:45 a.m. Wednesday.
