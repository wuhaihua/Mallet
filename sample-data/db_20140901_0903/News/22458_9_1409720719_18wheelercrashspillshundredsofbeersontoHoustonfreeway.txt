18-wheeler crash spills hundreds of beers onto Houston freeway 18-wheeler crash spills hundreds of beers onto Houston freeway A collision between an 18-wheeler and a car left dozens of cases of beer strewn across a Houston freeway early Tuesday morning.According to CBS affiliate KHOU, the accident happened at about 12:30 a.m. on the Katy Freeway. The 18-wheeler jackknifed, spilling dozens of cases of beer across the road's lanes.The driver of the 18-wheeler was in serious condition, the station reported. Authorities are investigating the cause of the accident.KHOU reports that it took about five hours to clean up the mess.Last month, a semi truck loaded with beer crashed in Indiana, spilling part of its load on Interstate 65 in Indiana.
