U.S. Launches Operation Against Islamist Rebels in Somalia U.S. Launches Operation Against Islamist Rebels in Somalia 1 hours                                                                             
											                                                                          The U.S. military conducted an operation in Somalia against the al-Qaeda-affiliated al-Shabaab network Monday, the Pentagon said. It offered no other details on the operation, which came a day after the Islamist rebels attacked government facilities in the capital, Mogadishu.&quot;We are assessing the results of the operation and will provide additional information as and when appropriate,&quot; Rear Adm. John Kirby, the Pentagon's press secretary, said in a statement late Monday.Al-Shabaab seized much of southern Somalia in 2006 before Somali forces and African peacekeeping troops ousted it five years later. The rebels, who still hold some towns, attacked a prison where dozens of its members are held on Sunday, with unconfirmed reports that 10 people were killed.
