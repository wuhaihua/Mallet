Police Hunt for SUV in Fatal Kansas City Shooting Case Police Hunt for SUV in Fatal Kansas City Shooting Case 1 hours                                                                             
											                                                                          Police discovered three people shot to death at a house in south Kansas City, Missouri, just before 1 p.m. Tuesday afternoon after responding to a disturbance call. Two others were found injured and transported to local hospitals with life-threatening injuries, police said.Authorities were looking for an unidentified person driving a beige SUV in connection with the investigation and officers were going door-to-door looking for a suspect, police Sgt. Kari Thompson told NBC affiliate KSHB. The unidentified victims of the shooting were discovered in multiple locations on a cul-de-sac in the area of Woodbridge Lane and Wornall Road, police told the station.SOCIAL
