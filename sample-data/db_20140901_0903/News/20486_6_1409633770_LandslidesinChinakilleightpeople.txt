Landslides in China kill eight people Landslides in China kill eight people 
					Beijing: At least eight people were killed and 24 missing on Tuesday after heavy rains caused landslides in China's Chongqing province.Rescue teams, including fire-fighters and police, have evacuated over 7,000 residents to safe locations Xinhua reported.The missing include 11 coal miners in Yunyang county who were buried due to a landslide on Monday.Heavy rains started in Yunyang, Wuxi, Fengjie and Wushan in Chongqing on Sunday, disrupting traffic and cutting off power supply and communication lines.IANS
